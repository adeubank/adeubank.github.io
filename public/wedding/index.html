The Start

It started with a glance three years ago. Allen had come into the diner that Vennesa worked at on a Friday night with his buddies. It was by chance that Allen had sat at Vennesa's table. They spent the night flirting and had an interest in each other. Allen desperately tried to get a date. Vennesa made Allen wait a year before they finally had their first date.

Next

The Fall

Upon meeting Allen, Vennesa knew he was the one. She was so intrigued with his intelligence and computer skills. Their first date was at a bar in Fullerton, where they talked the night away. On that night, they planned their first adventure: The Wedding. Just Kidding. They really planned a wine tasting trip.

Next

The Proposal

They continued dating, trying to spend as much time as they could with each other. They traveled to places like Napa, Temecula, San Francisco, and New Orleans. It was on December 13, 2013 when Allen proposed to Vennesa. It was their one year anniversary. Vennesa was so happy that she almost forgot to say yes.

And now they are tying the knot!!!
